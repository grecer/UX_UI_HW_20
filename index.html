Nonprofit Website Redesign

Making a nonprofit website responsive and more accessible to users through a full UX Redesign.

Team of 5, project delivered in 3 weeks

Role: Researcher, UX Designer

Deliverables:
  • Slide Deck containing research results and design process
  • Finished Figma prototype for both desktop and mobile versions of the website
Tools:
  • Figma
  • Google Suite
  • Slack
  • Pinterest
  • Procreate
